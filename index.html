Ledger.com/Start: Your Gateway to Secure Crypto Management

Ledger devices are trusted by millions worldwide for managing and safeguarding cryptocurrencies. If you're setting up your Ledger hardware wallet, the first step is visiting Ledger.com/start. This page serves as the official guide to help you configure your device, ensuring the highest level of security for your digital assets.

How to Begin with Ledger.com/Start

  1. Visit the Official Page:
    Navigate to Ledger.com/start using a secure and updated browser. Always ensure you’re on the official site to avoid phishing attempts.

  2. Download Ledger Live:
    On the page, you’ll find a download link for Ledger Live, the companion software for managing your Ledger device. Download the app for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux) or mobile device (iOS and Android).

  3. Set Up Your Ledger Wallet:
    Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Ledger device. This process includes:

    • Connecting your device to your computer or smartphone.
    • Choosing a secure PIN code.
    • Writing down your 24-word recovery phrase.

  4. Verify Your Device’s Authenticity:
    As part of the setup, Ledger ensures your device is genuine and hasn’t been tampered with. This step guarantees the safety of your crypto holdings.

  5. Start Managing Cryptocurrencies:
    Once set up, use Ledger Live to add accounts, monitor your portfolio, and send or receive cryptocurrencies securely.
